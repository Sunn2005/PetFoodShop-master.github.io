About this item:

Centropyge angels have forever been a favorite with hobbyists due to their smaller adult size, colorations and hardiness. The Coral Beauty Angel is one of the best recommendations for dwarf angels. These are among the least aggressive and have the easiest care of dwarf angelfish. The Coral Beauty can be housed with others of the same species as long as all of them are added at once and the aquarium is large enough. While it is one of the better bets in the Centropyge genus for reef aquariums, it can still be a risk, especially with clams. The larger the aquarium and the more often the fish are fed, the less likely you are to encounter this issue. Provide an environment with ample live rock for hiding and grazing and swimming space. The Coral Beauty has an adult size of about 4", which makes it ideal for smaller aquariums. Their body is a deep blue, which highlighted with oranges, reds and yellows throughout.
