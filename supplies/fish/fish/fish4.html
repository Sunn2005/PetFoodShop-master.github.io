About this item:

Centropyge angels have forever been a favorite with hobbyists due to their smaller adult size, colorations and hardiness. Bicolor Angels stand out not only because of their stark coloration of yellow and deep blue, but they also have a lot of personality. They are still very hardy and eat just about anything. Their small adult size of 6" means they can go into aquariums as small as 70 gallons. Like any other dwarf angel, they can be aggressive with other small angels and similarly shaped/colored fish. These should take just about anything you want to feed them, but we suggest a varied diet, ideally fed at least 2x per day. The Bicolor Angel is found singly in rubbly areas of lagoons and reef slopes up to 25 meters deep. They will use crevices in the reefs as shelter. They are found in the Western Pacific and range from Malaysia, north of Japan to south of Australia and east to Fiji.
